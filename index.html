ISTJ-ISFJ Comparative relations

This relationship has unbelievable similarities. They talk about similar things, have similar interests, behave according to the standards of courtesy and favor on each other, but they do not show interest in the problems of their opponents. Over time, this relationship becomes boring and stagnant. When they are at the same level in the hierarchy, they peacefully coexist, but if the other opponent is placed in a superior position to the other, they are in serious conflict and disagreement. They see the same problem from a very different perspective. If the opponent tries to solve the problem as they think, the other party thinks it is unrealistic. Moreover, they do not like to admit their viewpoints, and this leads to a huge misunderstanding. Especially when applying the method of work, it causes misunderstanding and consequently compromise.

 

 

 

In the company, their relationship is further developed. The reason is that the opponent 's behavior looks interesting and valuable to all the other opponents. They also have a good chance of improving their social relations a little more. When each other seeks advice, the person who advises finds that his advice is very useful, but the person seeking advice usually does not have anything left. Although they regard each other as selfish and self-centered, they do not want to convey this viewpoint to the other. In a family relationship this relationship is very heavy. They make distrust between each other and do not acknowledge their own meaning.
